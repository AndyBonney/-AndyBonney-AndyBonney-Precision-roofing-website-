Flat Roofing FAQs

How long does a flat roof last?

The lifespan of a flat roof depends on the material used and maintenance, but many systems last 15–25 years or more when properly installed.

How do I know if my flat roof needs replacing?

Signs include persistent leaks, pooling water, cracks, blisters, or visible wear. We can inspect your roof and advise whether a repair or replacement is best.

Can you repair a leaking flat roof?

Yes, many flat roof leaks can be repaired quickly and effectively. Early repairs help prevent more costly damage.

Do you install new flat roofs on extensions and garages?

Absolutely. We install durable flat roofing systems for home extensions, garages, porches and other structures.
