Common Signs Your Chimney Needs Repair

  • Water leaks or damp patches near your chimney breast
  • Loose, cracked or missing mortar between bricks
  • Lead flashing lifting or splitting
  • A leaning or unstable chimney stack
  • Debris falling into the guttering or onto the roof

If you notice any of these warning signs, our team can provide a professional inspection and recommend the right repair before the damage worsens.
