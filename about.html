Your Local Family Roofing Company

Precision Roofing is a trusted, family-run roofing company based in Stevenage, proudly serving homeowners across Hertfordshire and surrounding areas since 2016. We are known locally for our honest advice, reliable service and high-quality workmanship.

We understand how important your home is, which is why we treat every roofing job with care and attention to detail. Whether it’s a small repair or ongoing property maintenance, our goal is always to deliver long-lasting results at fair and competitive prices.

Our team specialises in roof repairs, flat roofing, and guttering, fascias and soffits as well as chimney work. We work on all types of residential properties and are happy to provide free inspections and no-obligation quotes.

As a local Stevenage roofing company, much of our work comes from word-of-mouth recommendations and repeat customers. We believe in doing the job properly the first time and building long-term trust with every customer we work with.
