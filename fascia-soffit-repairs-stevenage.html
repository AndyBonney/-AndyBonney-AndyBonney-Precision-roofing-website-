Guttering & Roofline FAQs

How do I know if my gutters need replacing?

Signs include frequent leaks, sagging sections, rust, cracks or water overflowing during rain. We can inspect and advise on the best solution.

How often should gutters be cleaned?

Gutters should usually be cleaned at least once a year to prevent blockages and water damage.

What are fascias and soffits?

Fascias and soffits are the boards along the edge of your roof that support the guttering and help protect the roof structure from weather and pests.

Do you replace old wooden fascias with uPVC?

Yes, we replace ageing or rotting timber roofline boards with durable, low-maintenance uPVC options.
